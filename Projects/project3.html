Star Wars: Episode III – Revenge of the Sith

Movie Description

Star Wars: Episode III - Revenge of the Sith is the final film in the prequel trilogy, depicting the end of the Clone Wars and the fall of the Jedi Order. It chronicles Jedi hero Anakin Skywalker's seduction to the dark side by Chancellor Palpatine, transforming him into Darth Vader. The film shows the Galactic Republic crumbling and the rise of the Galactic Empire, leading to a final confrontation between Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin.
