Information Technology

Information Technology(ITEC) is the multidisciplinary study, design , development, implementation, support or managment of computer-based Information Systems.

At GGC, we offer Bachelor of Sciencedegree in information technology.

Concentrations

Information Technology Department provides 5 concentrations:

Degital Media
This concentration is ideally suited for jobs involving digital media.

Enterprise Systems
This concentration is for students interested in information technology positions in business.

Software Development
This concentration is ideally suited for jobs in software development

Systems and Security
This concentration is ideally suited for jobs involving system networks and security of those systems.

Data Science and Analytics
This concentration is a data-driven program applying mathematics, computing and domain knowledge.
